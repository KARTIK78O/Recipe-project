Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  1. Sugar
  2. Bread
  3. Oil

Recipe

  • Soak bread in milk and knead.
  • Make small balls and deep fry them.
  • Prepare sugar syrup.
  • Dip fried balls in syrup.
  • Serve warm.

Dal Makhani

Ingredients

  1. Black Lentils
  2. Butter
  3. Cream

Recipe

  • Soak and boil dal.
  • Prepare tomato onion masala.
  • Add dal and cook with butter.
  • Simmer and add cream.
  • Serve with naan or rice.
